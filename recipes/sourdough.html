Baked Sourdough

A basic white sourdough, with just four ingredients:

Ingredients:

  • Flour: 600 g
  • Water: 380 g, 36 degrees Celsius
  • Salt: 12 g
  • Sourdough starter (natural yeast): 30 g

Steps

  1. Mix flour and water and let rest for 20 minutes
  2. Add the salt and sourdough starter to the mix and use the "pincer" method
  3. Let rest for 10 minutes
  4. Every 10 minutes "fold" the dough, and let rest, repeating four times
  5. Let proof overnight
  6. After about 8 - 10 hours, the dough should be ready to be shaped
  7. Take the dough out of the bowl on to a lightly dusted counter, and shape into a round
  8. Dust the banaton and once shaped, place the dough into it, bottom side down
  9. Let proof for another 2 - 4 hours, 40 minutes before it's ready, heat oven to 250 degrees Celsius
  10. Carefully take the dough out of the banaton and place on a dusted tray ready to put into the over
  11. Score the bread with your knife
  12. Place in the oven, reduce temperature to 210 degrees Celsius
  13. Set timer for 30 minutes, to check the bread
  14. After the 30 minutes, check and turn if required, determine how much longer is required (5 - 15 extra minutes)
  15. Once ready, take out of the oven and let cool on a rack
